Power BI Implementation

After scoping and prioritizing design efforts, I worked closely with developers to implement new design features in PowerBI and customize existing components. I also modeled datasets based on new visualizations and prepared versions of the application for further usability testing.

Prototyping and Interaction Design

To share proposed interactions with the design team and business stakeholders I made click-through prototypes from the static visual designs using InVision. This allowed me to iterate rapidly on new designs based on feedback from the team and other stakeholders.

Using a combination of Sketch, Zeplin, and Power BI, I designed wireframes, mockups, and style guides to facilitate rapid design and development of customized data visualization and UI components. I also created a custom JSON style pallet for PowerBI to adhere to PwC's brand guidelines and style and vetted other sources of custom PowerBI visualizations.

Design

Based on an exploratory round of testing, I designed test plans and several dashboard layouts to test the discoverability of UI features for filtering and highlighting data and the appropriate terminology to match our users real-world expectations. I conducted 2 studies with 4 participants from our target user group. Metrics gathered included completion rates, time to completion, subjective rating and other observations.

PwC Data Visualization

I consulted for PricewaterhouseCoopers in the summer of 2017, conducting research and design for the development of data visualization tools to support partner level managers and directors across several countries. As part of a larger team, I designed and conducted several rounds of usability testing, provided specialized data visualization training, and designed and developed user interfaces using Sketch and PowerBI. I also assisted in the creation of a visual style guide that would assist in future design iterations.

Impact

  • Gathered, analyzed, and interpreted data
  • Reported findings and design recommendations
  • Implemented customized design solutions
  • Provided specialized data visualization training to development team

My contributions to our project helped define user requirements and created a baseline for usability and measuring progress during design sprints.
