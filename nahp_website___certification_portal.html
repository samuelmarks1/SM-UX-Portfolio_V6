User Research and Artifact Analysis

Meeting regularly with NAHP employees and administrators,  our team conducted interviews, contextual inquiries, and other research exercises. This helped us to understand internal workflow processes, identify archetype users, and develop user requirements for design. Certification applications and exams were particularly cumbersome for students, examiners, and the administrators that managed them. Both processes were thoroughly evaluated and redesigned for usability.

Wireframes and Design

From the information architecture outlined in feature requirements documents, I used a combination of hand-sketching, Adobe XD and Sketch to design wireframes, components, and mockups. Involving stakeholders in the process helped to identify inconsistencies between the approved workflow and UI, as well as issues with brand and styling.

Prototyping and Interaction Design

Where mockups and storyboards fell short complex user flows were demonstrated using interactive prototypes. I used the Craft plugin for Sketch to prototype and upload design screens to InVision. After gaining buy-in on specific features, I uploaded the approved screens and components to Zeplin to share with developers.

Information Architecture

Many contingencies within the clients' internal processes were hidden from users and were regulated by psychometric testing recommendations. The new information architecture chunked complex workflows and information into UIs that are relevant to user's goals.

  • Updated company logo and created brand and style guidelines.
  • Automated 8+ workflow processes previously done manually on paper.
  • Created e-commerce site for merchandise and exam materials

Due to their newly designed website and certification portal our clients' workload was significantly reduced, showing an immediate measurable return on investment

NAHP Website & Certification Portal

The National Association for Health Professionals posed a unique challenge  to visually redesign their website and create a certification portal to automate and manage applications and fees, exams, exam scores, and ongoing certification criteria. As Lead UX Designer on the project I conducted user research and data analysis, created information architecture, wireframes, mockups, styleguides, and interactive prototypes.
